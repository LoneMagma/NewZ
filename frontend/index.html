NewZ
Version 3.0 — open source
NewZ is a self-hosted news reader that understands who you are.

It pulls from reputable Indian and global sources every five minutes, scores every article against your profile using an AI layer, and surfaces only what's actually relevant to you — no algorithmic feed, no ads, no noise.

The quiet presence at the top is Pacify — it reads your feed and tells you what matters at a glance.
